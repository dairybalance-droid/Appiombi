# Legal Review Checklist

This checklist is for legal and privacy validation before production release.

## Corporate And Contractual

- confirm provider legal entity details
- confirm governing law and jurisdiction
- confirm B2B/B2F framing if relevant
- confirm limitation of liability text

## Privacy Policy

- confirm controller/processor roles
- confirm lawful bases
- confirm retention periods
- confirm data subject rights handling
- confirm privacy contact details
- confirm export and deletion wording

## Terms Of Service

- confirm service description
- confirm professional-use disclaimer
- confirm non-substitution of veterinary judgment
- confirm subscription and suspension clauses
- confirm farm data ownership clauses
- confirm user responsibility for data entered

## Tracking / Cookie Policy

- confirm whether any analytics or marketing SDKs are used
- confirm whether consent is required
- confirm tracking categories and retention

## DPA / Vendor Chain

- confirm sub-processors
- confirm hosting region
- confirm transfer safeguards if non-EU processing exists
- confirm TOMs and incident obligations

## Product Behaviors To Validate

- required privacy acceptance on registration
- required terms acceptance on registration
- re-consent on new active versions
- operator invitation and revocation
- subscription read-only behavior
- account suspension behavior
- data export availability where legally required
